IT Scholarship Project

The IDB-BISEW IT Scholarship Project was launched in 2003 with the aim of enhancing the employment prospects of meritorious Muslim youths who come from financially disadvantaged background.

Read More

Vocational Project

IDB-BISEW is targeting a particularly neglected and vulnerable section of the student population of the country. These are the students of underprivileged background who fail to progress from high school to the HSC levels.

Read More

Madrasah Project

IDB-BISEW has taken up the initiative of providing vocational education and training to the students of selected Madrasahs by exposing them to vocational education/training in the trades of their choice.

Read More

Orphanage Project

IDB-BISEW has established a two-storied orphanage named 'Khaiyarbhanga Nuria Hafizia Eatimkhana' in the district of Madaripur. This orphanage has a capacity of providing active services to 100 orphans at a time.

Read More

Project Objective

The purpose of the project is to provide scholarship to underprivileged but meritorious Muslim Youths to undergo extensive training for development of employable skills in the field of Information and Communication Technology (ICT). These unemployed graduates possess enormous potential which could not yet be properly exploited due to lack of finance.

Who can apply for the IDB-BISEW scholarships?

Candidates who have a strong desire to build themselves up as IT professionals but do not have the financial means to pay for the required training. They must have the following academic backgrounds:

  • Bachelors/Fazil degree holders
  • Diploma holders in Civil Engineering or Architecture
  • Candidates must have at least second division or GPA 2.25 results in their SSC and HSC examinationsApply Online

    Objectives of Vocational training

    It has been observed that many students in Bangladesh, after completing their SSC/Dakhil exam, cannot pursue their further education due to financial crisis. Hence, IDB-BISEW has taken initiatives to turn this huge manpower into productive workforce through providing employable technical skills.

    Application Criteria

  • Class VIII pass or SSC/Dakhil pass but inability to pursue further education due to economic constraint.
  • Age not more than 25
